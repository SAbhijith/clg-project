VKCET College of Engineering and Technology

(Approved by AICTE and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University) is an auspicious venture of Valia Koonambaikulam Sreebhadrakali kshetra Trust, Vadakkevila P.O., Kollam, Kerala - an established charitable religious trust. The college is situated in Chavarcode, Parippally, Kerala. The college is established with the object of providing quality education to deserving students on merit without any barriers and concessions to the poor students in the most demanding branches of engineering.

"Admission Open for 2022-23"

50% of the seats are under Government Quota, allotment to the same will be made by the Commissioner of Entrance Examinations (CEE Kerala). The remaining 50% of seats are under Management Quota and the admission will be made strictly based on merit, which includes NRI / PIO seats also. Applications for admission to the B.Tech. Programme during the academic year 2022-23 under Management Quota, NRI, and PIO are now open. Applications can be "submitted online".
